HOI AN TRAVEL TOUR GUIDE

Tra Que Organic Vegetable Village & Bamboo Basket Boat with Meal (price :700.000)

Picking up time :
8.30 am to 1.00pm (morning tour)
1.30 pm to 6.00pm (afternoon tour)
This trip for those who love to discover rural life, want to immerse themselves into the atmosphere of peaceful, rustic landscapes and get closer to the nature, friendly people in the Vietnam countryside

Details:

Pickup

Our guides meet you up at the Meeting Point (Godiva - Dinks & Cakes at 11 Hoang Dieu Street, Hoi An )
Then, you move to Tra Que Vegetable Village by bicycles. You have a chance to feel the green with distinct aroma of local varieties of vegetables here. Then, you will be guided the whole process of growing vegetables as real farmers.
Saying goodbye to Tra Que Vegetable Village, you will ride to Cam Thanh Eco -Village. On the way, you will contemplate the beauty of countryside in Vietnam. You will have an opportunity to stop and take many beautiful photos of rice paddies and some animals in Viet Nam countryside such as cow, water buffalo, stock, chicken,duck….

Action

After that, you move to Water- coconut jungle and have chance to rowing basket boats with the locals. You will be immersed in the natural beauty of the nipa palm forest. You also will know some traditional ways of catching fish as well as the lives of the hospitable fishermen.
The final stop is our restaurant. You have lunch or dinner with some Vietnamese foods and take a rest there. Go back to the Meeting Point. End of the tour!
